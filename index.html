About

Many roles.
One presence.

My name is Sanja, and I'm an actress based in Stockholm. I have experience in film and television, including roles in several well-known series.

My main focus is screen acting. I'm especially drawn to emotionally charged scenes, complex characters and material with strong subtext. I enjoy working with vulnerability, tension and intensity, and I'm comfortable expressing emotion with both subtlety and presence on camera.

I have trained in film acting at Cinemantrix and taken courses at Calle Flygare. I'm currently in my third term of improvisational theatre, and this autumn I will continue with a six-month film acting course at Cinemantrix. I'm confident with both scripted material and improvisation, take direction well, and adapt easily to the tone and rhythm of a production.

Outside of acting, I'm very active and have a background in climbing and dance. This makes me comfortable with movement-based work and physically demanding roles, including scenes that require body awareness, coordination and intensity.

I speak Swedish, English and Serbo-Croatian fluently, with working proficiency in Spanish.
